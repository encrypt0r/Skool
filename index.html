Erbil Polytechnic University

Erbil Polytechnic University is a public polytechnic university in Erbil, Kurdistan founded in 1993.

Engineering College

Engineering College includes these departments:

  • Civil
  • Highway
  • Information Systems
  • Mechanic and Energy

Administrative College

Administrative College is another college of EPU. It includes these departments:

  • Accounting department
  • Adminstration department

Health college

Engineering College includes these departments:

  • Laboratory Department
  • Psychiatric department
  • Department of Neuroscience
  • Nursing department
